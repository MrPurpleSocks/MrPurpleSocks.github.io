Workload

Honestly there is A LOT of work, both at home and at school, when you go to VCS. It could be because one, or more, teachers could assign a lot of work, like Mrs. Douglas, and you might not have time to finish it. But, you could also, I'm being honest right now, You could be lazy and not want to do the work. and don't think your the only one that does it, quite a few people I know do it at least some times, I also sometimes do it.

Boy's PE Grade 7/8

Teacher: Coach Coleman

Grade: 8/10

Reasons:

Most of the class was pretty good, there were some units that were less fun than others. The main one that I didn't like was the track unit. My favorite unit was the swim unit, it was the eaisest unit and the most fun. And, the teacher is a great coach, and liked teaching the students.

Would I recommend this class to another Student?

International Space Station

Teacher: Mrs. Kraver

Grade: 10/10

Reasons:

If you like technology, like Arduino, and the ISS then you will love ths class.If you don't like technolagy, specificly circtry, then you WILL have a hard time in this class. The teacher Ms. Kraver loves teaching. If you want to learn circutry then you might want to take this class. Also, as long as you can finish the circut thanyou will get full credit. Note: you will have to "Trace the circut" by showing how it works on video.

Would I recommend this class to another Student?

I think it depends on the student, you would have to read the above reasons to find out if it fits you.
